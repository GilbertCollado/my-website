Dominican Breakfast

Tres Golpes

A traditional Dominican breakfast consisting of mashed plantains (mangú), fried eggs, fried cheese, and Dominican salami.

Other Popular Breakfast Options

  • Pan de agua with butter and cheese
  • Hot chocolate and bread
  • Arepitas (fried cornmeal fritters)
  • Morir Soñando (milk and orange juice drink)
