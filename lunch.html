Top 10 Dominican Lunch Dishes

Classic midday eats from the DR

  1. La Bandera Dominicana

  2. Sancocho (7-Meat Stew)

    A hearty stew of seven meats, tubers, and vegetables. The ultimate comfort food. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}

  3. Mofongo

    Mashed plantains with garlic and chicharrón, often served with meat or seafood. :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}

  4. Pollo Guisado

  5. Habichuelas Guisadas

  6. Locrio de Pollo

    One-pot chicken and rice dish, flavored with vegetables and sazón. :contentReference[oaicite:6]{index=6}

  7. Pescado Frito
