Dominican Lunch

La Bandera

The most traditional Dominican lunch consisting of white rice, stewed beans, meat (usually chicken or beef), and salad.

Other Common Lunches

  • Mofongo with meat or seafood
  • Locrio (Dominican-style rice with chicken or sausage)
  • Sancocho (hearty stew with root vegetables and meats)
