Dominican Drinks

Morir Soñando

A refreshing drink made with orange juice, milk, sugar, and ice, known for its dreamy, creamy flavor.

Jugo de Chinola

Passion fruit juice, tart and sweet, often served cold with sugar and water.

Jugo de Tamarindo

Tamarind juice made from the tangy pulp of the tamarind pod, mixed with water and sugar.

Limonada

Classic lemonade made with fresh lime juice, water, and sugar, often served icy cold.
