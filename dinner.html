Dominican Dinner

Sancocho

A rich stew made from several types of meat and root vegetables. Served during special occasions or Sundays with rice and avocado.

Other Common Dinner Dishes

  • Pollo Guisado (stewed chicken)
  • Moro de Guandules (rice with pigeon peas)
  • Fried fish with tostones and salad
