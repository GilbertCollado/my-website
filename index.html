Welcome

My name is Gilbert Collado. I was born in the Dominican Republic and raised in the United States. This website is my way of sharing my culture with you through the flavors and stories behind our food.

In the Dominican Republic, meals are more than just food. They’re family gatherings, Sunday traditions, and shared memories. Whether it's a simple breakfast of mashed plantains or a stew that simmers for hours, every dish has a story and I invite you to discover them.

More Than a Meal

Dominican cuisine blends African, Taino, and Spanish influences into something uniquely ours. We eat with joy, cook with love, and serve with pride. Food is how we stay connected to our heritage and now, I hope it helps you connect to it, too.
