Dominican Cuisine

Written by Gilbert Collado

Certified Platano Connoisseur

Bienvenidos to the world of Dominican dishes and their recipes. This website will teach you about the best dishes in the Dominican Republic.

Top Dishes

  • Tres Golpes (Breakfast)
  • La Bandera (Lunch)
  • Sancocho (Dinner)
  • Mofongo

Breakfast

Tres Golpes: A combo of mashed plantains, fried cheese, salami, and eggs.

Lunch

La Bandera: Rice, red beans, and stewed meat. Often served with salad and fried plantains.

Dinner

Sancocho: A hearty Dominican stew made with various meats, root vegetables, and spices. It’s often enjoyed for special occasions or family gatherings.
